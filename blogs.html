What do you want to learn?

Select a software below to explore our collection of blogs and tutorials, designed to help you master the tools and technologies we use in Mayavi.

Unity

Unity is a powerful and versatile game development engine that allows you to create and play games across various platforms. It also involves sharing knowledge and guiding others in using the engine's features to bring their creative ideas to life.

SparkAR Studio

SparkAR Studio is a platform that enables you to create augmented reality effects and filters for social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

Unreal & Core

Unreal Engine and Core offer extensive tools, documentation, and support, empowering you to build complex games and interactive content while fostering a vibrant community.

Blender

Blender is a versatile and feature-rich 3D modeling and animation software that allows you to create stunning visuals and realistic simulations.

Lens Studio

Lens Studio is a user-friendly platform that allows you to create augmented reality experiences and filters for platforms like Snapchat.

Google AR Core

Google AR Core enables you to experience augmented reality on supported Android devices, bringing virtual objects into the real world through your smartphone or tablet.
