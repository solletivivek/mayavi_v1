Welcome To Mayavi

Mayavi is a Technology club of K L University where students collaborate to work on Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, Game development, Mixed Reality.

Our Wings

We are excited to announce that in our club, we will ensure to share and explore all the wings of gaming, virtual reality, augmented reality, and mixed reality with our members

Game Development

Game development is a captivating blend of artistry and technology, where worlds come alive and stories unfold. It encompasses the process of creating video games from concept to completion, engaging players in immersive experiences.

Extended Reality

Extended Reality (XR) is a revolutionary technology that merges the real and virtual worlds, creating immersive and interactive experiences. It encompasses Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR), blurring the boundaries between the physical and digital realms.

Modelling and Animations

Modelling and animations are crucial aspects of digital content creation, bringing life and realism to virtual worlds. Modelling involves creating 3D models of characters, objects, and environments using specialized software. Skilled artists meticulously craft the shape, texture, and details of these models, ensuring accuracy and visual appeal.

UI/UX

UI/UX, short for User Interface and User Experience, focuses on creating seamless and engaging digital interactions. UI encompasses the visual elements and layout of an interface, including buttons, menus, and navigation, while UX focuses on the overall user journey and experience. A well-designed UI/UX strives to provide intuitive navigation

Tools

The tools related to our club encompass creativity, innovation, design, and other diverse modes that are indispensable.

Unity

Unity is a powerful and versatile game development engine that allows to create and play games across various platforms. It also involves sharing knowledge and guiding others in using the engine's features to bring their creative ideas to life.

SparkAR Studio

SparkAR Studio is a platform that enables to create augmented reality effects and filters for social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

Unreal & Core

Unreal Engine and Core offer extensive tools, documentation, and support, empowering to build complex games and interactive content while fostering a vibrant community.

Blender

Blender is a versatile and feature-rich 3D modeling and animation software that allows to create stunning visuals and realistic simulations.

Lens Studio

Lens Studio is a user-friendly platform that allows to create augmented reality experiences and filters for platforms like Snapchat.

Google AR Core

Google AR Core enables to experience augmented reality on supported Android devices, bringing virtual objects into the real world through my smartphone or tablet.

Welcome to the Mayavi Technology Club

At Mayavi, we believe that gaming is not just a hobby but an art form that can be mastered. Our club is dedicated to creating an immersive and inclusive environment where enthusiasts like you can come together, share ideas, and collaborate on exciting projects.By joining Mayavi, you open yourself up to a world of opportunities and benefits.

AR Development

VR Development

Embark on a journey into Virtual Reality (VR) with Mayavi. Build captivating virtual environments, develop interactive gameplay mechanics, and transport users to extraordinary realms.

UI/UX Designing
